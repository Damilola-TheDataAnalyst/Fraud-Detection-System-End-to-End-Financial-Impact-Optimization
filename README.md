# Fraud-Detection-System-End-to-End-Financial-Impact-Optimization
Enterprise-grade fraud detection pipeline using XGBoost/LightGBM, SHAP explainability, and financial impact optimization to minimize losses &amp; preserve revenue.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Phase 1 — Define the Business Problem (FinTech Data Analyst)

Objective:
Develop a data-driven fraud detection system that maximizes fraud capture while minimizing disruption to legitimate customers, thereby protecting both revenue and reputation.

Why it matters:

In FinTech, blocking legitimate transactions (false positives) can erode customer trust and reduce revenue. At the same time, missed fraud (false negatives) leads to direct financial losses. Our system must strike an optimal balance.

Key Metrics to Optimize:

1. Fraud Recall (Sensitivity): Maximize detection of fraudulent transactions to reduce losses.

2. Precision: Ensure genuine customers are not incorrectly flagged, preserving revenue.

3. Financial Impact: Quantify both losses prevented and revenue preserved to demonstrate ROI.

Before any modeling, I will:

1. Understand transaction flow: Identify high-risk behaviors, payment types, and patterns leading to fraud.

2. Define fraud scenarios: Card-not-present, account takeover, chargeback-prone behaviors, etc.

3. Align with business goals: Collaborate with Finance and Risk teams to quantify the cost of fraud vs. cost of false positives.

4. Set measurable success criteria: Establish thresholds for recall, precision, and financial impact metrics.
